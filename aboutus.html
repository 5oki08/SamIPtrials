Umoja Cleaning ServicesTM is an organisation based in Kenya that offers a wide variety of cleaning services. Our company was started in 2000, with three friends who birthed the idea in their small house somewhere in the outskirts of Nairobi. They started off with houses, their neighbours', then as they grew their name, went on to social halls, then churches and currently working with even buildings and companies.
